Footmark is Cross Devices Solution

Digitalizing Every Square Foot of Land Layout – Securely, Smartly, Seamlessly.

Footmark is dedicated to revolutionizing the real estate industry by digitalizing every square foot of land. We provide secure, smart, and seamless solutions for land plot management.

Our platform offers real-time visualization of plot layouts, allowing agents, admins, and customers to see up-to-the-minute status updates. This eliminates double-bookings and ensures perfect data consistency across all users and devices.

Powerful Features for Modern Land Layout Management

Everything you need to manage land plots efficiently and securely

Interactive Plot Layouts

View and manage plots with real-time color-coded status indicators for available, booked, hold, sold, and blocked properties.

Real-Time Synchronization

Multi-user updates sync instantly across devices, preventing double-bookings and ensuring data consistency.

Role-Based Access Control

Secure authentication with distinct permissions for managers, admins, agents, and customers.

Multi-User Collaboration

Enable teams to work together seamlessly with concurrent access and automatic conflict resolution.
